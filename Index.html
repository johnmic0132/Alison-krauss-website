Biography

Alison Krauss is an internationally acclaimed American bluegrass-country singer, violinist, and producer. With an extraordinary career spanning decades, she has earned numerous Grammy Awards, making her one of the most awarded female artists in history. Known for her angelic voice and timeless music, Alison continues to inspire audiences around the world with her artistry and passion.
